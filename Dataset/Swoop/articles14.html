CITY DRAMA

Dundee man arrested over ‘firearms possession’ after armed cops swoop on ‘road rage incident’

  • Ewan Mowat
  • 19:32, 20 Jul 2021
  • Updated: 19:32, 20 Jul 2021

A MAN has been arrested in connection with a firearms incident following an alleged road rage rammy in Dundee.

Armed cops swooped on Old Glamis Road around 7.40pm last night.

Officers were responding to reports of a road rage incident in the north of the city.

However, it is alleged a 30-year-old man was in possession of a firearm when the specialist police teams arrived.

He was arrested in connection with the incident.

A Police Scotland spokesperson said: “Police received report of a road rage incident and subsequently, a man allegedly in possession of a firearm, on Old Glamis Road in Dundee around 7.40pm on Monday July 19.

“Officers, including armed officers, attended and a 30-year-old man has been arrested in connection.”
